Who We Are

About ProHLP Solutions

A specialist Project Management & Technical Services company with three decades of combined senior experience.

PSPL partners with investors, developers, and hospitality groups to de-risk projects, optimise costs, and deliver world-class built environments on time and within budget. Our senior team has operated at leadership level within multinationals including Oberoi Hotels, AccorHotels, JLL, InterGlobe, Bovis Lend Lease, and SEMBCORP.

We bring depth across three key verticals — Hospitality, Commercial, and Residential — with an integrated team covering project management, MEP, green building, and technical services under one roof.

Single-Window Consultancy
Feasibility through handover — design, cost, quality, MEP and sustainability managed cohesively without coordination gaps.
Sector-Specific Depth
Focused exclusively on hospitality, commercial and residential — not generalist advisory. We understand the standards, operators and compliance requirements that define each sector.
Technology-Augmented Delivery
AI-enhanced project monitoring, BIM coordination and digital reporting for real-time intelligence and proactive decision-making on every project.

Service Offerings

End-to-end project management and technical consultancy across the full development lifecycle.

01
Feasibility Studies
Market & financial feasibility, operator selection, concept brief, site assessment and ROI modelling for new developments.
02
Due Diligence
Technical due diligence for acquisitions, operator mandates, legal & regulatory compliance and asset condition assessments.
03
Design Review
Design management, value engineering, drawing review, constructability assessments and operator brand compliance checks.
04
Cost Optimisation
Zero-based budgeting, cost-to-complete analysis, procurement strategy and systematic value engineering solutions.
05
Quality & HSE Audits
Site inspections, QA/QC plans, HSE compliance, IFC standards alignment and statutory safety management.
06
MEP Services Audit
MEP design review, systems audit, energy benchmarking, HVAC, plumbing and electrical oversight across all asset classes.
07
Project Audits
Quarterly health checks, progress reporting, risk registers, and independent time & cost tracking for project governance.
08
Green Building
LEED, IGBC, GRIHA & GEM certification, whole building energy simulation and sustainability audit services.
